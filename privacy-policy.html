Privacy Policy

How Rhine Media handles information submitted through this website.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you contact Rhine Media, we may collect the information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, company, Telegram or Skype handle, vertical, budget range, and message details.

2. How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to enquiries, evaluate potential cooperation, understand campaign requirements, and communicate with prospective partners.

3. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers or partners only when needed to respond to your request, evaluate a campaign opportunity, or comply with legal obligations.

4. Retention

We keep enquiry information only as long as reasonably needed for business communication, compliance, and record-keeping purposes.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect submitted information, but no online transmission or storage method can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

6. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at hello@rhine-media.com.
